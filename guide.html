Getting Started
First-time setup in under 2 minutes
1   Sign in with Google

Open corevado.netlify.app and tap Sign in with Google. Use your personal Google account — each person in your household has their own separate data.

Two people, one household. Each person signs in with their own Google account. Personal data (logs, fitness, goals) stays private. Shared data (tasks, inventory) is visible to both.
🔒   Your data is private and secure

Everything you enter in Corevado — your daily logs, fitness, banking, inventory, and finances — is stored in a private space tied to your household only. No one outside your household can see it, and it is never shared, sold, or made publicly visible in any way.

🏠
Household only
Only people in your household with the Household ID can access shared data
🔐
Personal stays personal
Your logs, health data, and goals are visible to you alone — not even your partner sees them
🏦
Banking is yours
Financial data lives in your private database — no third party has access to it
🚫
Closed system
Corevado does not share, sell, or expose your data outside your household
🛡️   Is my info safe?

Totally. Corevado stores all your data through Firebase — that's Google's secure cloud platform. Think of it like your data living in Google's vault.

It's encrypted, it's private to your account, and nobody else — including us — can see it. We don't sell your info or share it with anyone. Ever.
2   Complete the Setup Wizard

On first launch you'll see a short setup wizard. This takes about 60 seconds and unlocks the full app.

  1. Enter your first name (used in AI check-ins and daily summaries)
  2. Enter your partner's name (used in task assignments)
  3. Your Household ID is generated automatically when you complete setup — no action needed
What is the Household ID? It's a unique code that links two accounts together so you and your partner share the same tasks, inventory, and finances. Once your setup is complete, go to Settings and you'll see your Household ID. Share that code with your partner — when they sign in and enter it during their setup, both accounts are connected automatically. Each person still has their own private data (logs, fitness, goals) but everything household-related stays in sync.

Your Daily Rhythm
The core loop that keeps everything on track

Corevado is built around a simple loop: log in the morning, log in the evening, review on Sunday. Each check-in takes about 90 seconds. The app does the rest.

Morning — 90 seconds
Log your weight, sleep, energy, steps, and set your intention for the day. Opens the Personal tab → Morning.
Evening — 90 seconds
Rate your day, track water, log workouts, note your daily win. Opens the Personal tab → Evening.
During the day — as needed
Log workouts in Health → Train. Track meals in Health → Food. Check off tasks in Home. Update your inventory as you use or restock things.
Sunday — 5 minutes
Fill in the weekly review. Tap "Generate AI Brief" to get your 5-line accountability summary. Set intentions for the week ahead.
Consistency beats perfection. Even a partial log is better than none. The AI insights only get smarter the more you fill in — after 4 weeks you'll see real patterns.

Goals

Track any goal with a progress chart. Corevado includes templates for weight loss, debt payoff, savings, and custom habits. Each goal shows a progress bar and trend line pulled from your daily logs.

To add a goal: tap the + Add Goal button, pick a category, set your target and deadline. Weight goals automatically pull data from your morning check-ins.

Sunday review

Every Sunday, take 5 minutes to fill in a short reflection. You'll rate each of the 7 life pillars (Family, Faith, Fitness, Finance, Learning, Fun, and Marriage) and add any notes about the week.

When you're done, tap Generate AI Brief. The AI reads everything you filled in and writes a short, plain-English summary — what went well, what to watch, and one thing to focus on next week. Think of it as a weekly debrief written for you, not by you.

After several weeks, use History → Pattern Insights to run a deep AI analysis across all your Sunday reviews — it surfaces recurring cycles and blind spots you'd never spot manually.

Budget

Corevado uses envelope budgeting — you allocate a fixed amount to each spending category at the start of the month, then track how much you've used.

15+ envelopes are pre-configured (Groceries, Dining, Gas, Entertainment, Kids, etc.). Each shows a health bar: green when under, orange when near, red when over budget. Tap any envelope to see every transaction inside it.

Unspent money at month-end can be rolled over to the next month or reallocated. This is how you build buffer over time toward goals like debt payoff or savings.
Import Transactions

The fastest way to get your spending in. Corevado supports four import methods:

  1. Bank / credit card CSV — log in to your bank or credit card provider's website, download a statement as a CSV or XLSX file, and drag it into the Import tab. Most major banks offer this under Statements or Transaction History.
  2. Screenshot scan — take a screenshot of any bank statement and let the AI extract the transactions via OCR. Useful for banks without a CSV export option.
  3. Manual entry — add a single transaction by hand at any time.

After import, Corevado uses 47+ merchant rules to auto-categorize transactions into the right envelopes. Review and correct any that land in the wrong place — the app learns your corrections over time.

Your file is never stored. When you upload a CSV or screenshot, it is used once to read your transactions and then discarded immediately. Your raw bank file never lives inside Corevado — only the individual transaction records you choose to import are saved. Your sensitive banking information is always just a one-time touchpoint, never a permanent part of the app.
Insights

Sort and filter all your transactions 6 ways: by date, amount, merchant, envelope, card, or category. Use this to find recurring charges, spot unusual spending, or verify that a specific transaction was captured correctly.

AI Finance Coach

The coach reads up to 3 months of your spending data and writes a plain-English monthly brief: where your money actually went, how it compared to your budget, and one concrete suggestion for next month.

You can also ask it questions in the chat — "how much did we spend on dining last month?" or "are we on track for the debt payoff goal?" It has full access to your envelope and transaction data.

Tips & Power Features
Get the most out of Corevado
Logging vs. auto-save

The Morning and Evening check-ins require you to tap the Log button to save the day's entry — this is intentional so you consciously commit to the log before it's written.

Most other areas (inventory adjustments, task status, goal edits) save automatically 1.5 seconds after you make a change. You'll see a brief sync indicator at the bottom of the screen when data is written.

Offline mode

If your internet drops, Corevado keeps working using local storage on your device. All changes are held and synced automatically once you're back online. You'll see an Offline indicator at the bottom of the screen when this is active.

Dark & light mode

Corevado defaults to dark mode. To switch, go to the Settings tab (gear icon) and toggle the theme. The setting is saved per-device.
